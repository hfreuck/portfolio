Basic Income in Wisconsin Story

For one of my classes in spring 2021, I worked with classmate Zhen Wang to research and write a story about universal basic income programs in Wisconsin. Our final story "Wisconsin cities look to basic income to close racial, other wealth gaps," was published in Wisconsin Watch and the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel. It was also a part of our class series "The Color of Money," which was nominated for several awards, including being named a finalist for the "Best collaborative college/university & professional website" category in the Eppy Awards.
