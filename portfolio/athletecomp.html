Student-Athlete Compensation Story

In fall 2019, I wrote a story about the political debates around student-athlete compensation for college athletes at both the legislative and NCAA levels. The issue had gained traction for years, and in fall of 2019, several states started the process of passing legislation to allow student-athlete compensation. Today, student-athletes are allowed to agree to name, image, and likeness (NIL) deals while in college, a key turning point in the tensions between athletes and NCAA/university administrators.
