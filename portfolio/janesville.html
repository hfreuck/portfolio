Janesville City Survey

I wrote a story using a study from the City of Janesville, which found that one-third of the city's residents weren't perceived as welcoming to outsiders. While many people didn't view the city as welcoming to everyone, more than three-fourths of residents still believed that Janesville was a good place to live.
