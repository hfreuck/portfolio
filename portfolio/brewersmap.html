1982 Brewers Retro Map

In my project “1982 Brewers,” I used the 1982 AL Champion Milwaukee Brewers as inspiration to design a map on Mapbox Studio. Using the team’s color scheme, typeface, and other design aspects and icons relating to the team, I designed a map of the world that featured key points that embody Milwaukee’s culture, including stadiums and bars.
